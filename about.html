About MFABCON

MFABCON brings over 15 years of proven excellence in construction, fabrication, and engineering across the Middle East. With a team of highly skilled engineers, draftsmen, supervisors, and technicians, we deliver end-to-end project management—from concept and design to execution, testing, and commissioning.

We specialize in General Contracting, Steel Fabrication, HVAC, Civil & Architectural Works, and Electro-Mechanical Projects. Our focus is on achieving the highest standards of quality, safety, and client satisfaction in every step.

Committed to timely and cost-effective delivery, MFABCON manages both large-scale and turnkey projects with precision. We also provide annual maintenance, MEP services, remodeling, and façade enhancements. Guided by our vision of excellence, we combine innovation and craftsmanship to create lasting value for our clients.

Building the Future, One Project at a Time

Vision

To be the trusted strategic partner of choice, delivering innovative civil, industrial, and architectural solutions built on integrity, excellence, and sustainability.

Mission

To lead through technical expertise, innovation, and commitment to client satisfaction — upholding the highest standards of safety, quality, and ethical practice in every project we undertake.

Ethics & Compliance

Legal and ethical standards form the cornerstone of our integrity. At MFABCON, we maintain strict adherence to our Business Conduct Handbook, outlining policies on conflict of interest, workplace ethics, financial integrity, and safety. Our Supplier Code of Conduct ensures fair trade, ethical sourcing, and compliance with environmental and safety standards across all partnerships.

Health, Safety & Quality Assurance

At MFABCON, we are committed to ensuring a safe and secure work environment. We exert relentless effort to prevent workplace accidents and safeguard our people. Being OHSAS 18001 certified, we firmly believe that every incident and occupational injury is preventable.

On the Job

We reinforce technical procedures, continuous training, and enforce a Life Critical Requirements Program to manage high-risk activities safely. Our Project Managers, Leaders, and subcontractors are empowered to stop any unsafe work — if it’s not safe, we don’t do it.

Public Safety

Public safety is integral to our operations. We collaborate with stakeholders to enhance safety, health, and environmental standards across all project sites.

Quality Assurance

Quality drives every phase of our planning and execution. As an ISO 9001 certified company, MFABCON upholds rigorous quality control processes to ensure defect-free, timely, and compliant project delivery. We maintain active communication with subcontractors to implement effective inspection and quality control measures.
